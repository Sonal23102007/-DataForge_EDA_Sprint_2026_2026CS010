# Report
**1. What is the dashboard’s main business message?**
The dashboard highlights overall business performance by showing key metrics such as total sales, profit, and order count. It helps identify trends over time, top-performing categories, and areas where profitability is low. The main message is to provide a clear view of how the business is performing and where improvements or strategic decisions are needed.

**2. Which filter changes the story most strongly?**
The filter that changes the story most strongly is the **Category/Region (or Date)** filter. When this filter is applied, it significantly alters the insights by showing how different categories or regions contribute to sales and profit. For example, some categories may have high sales but low profit, which completely changes the interpretation of business performance.

**3. What would you improve if you had one more day?**
If I had one more day, I would improve the dashboard by:

* Adding more interactive visuals such as drill-through or tooltips for deeper insights
* Improving the design and layout for better readability and user experience
* Adding advanced KPIs like profit margin and growth rate
* Including a more detailed date analysis (monthly/quarterly trends)
* Ensuring better data storytelling with clear titles and insights for each visual
